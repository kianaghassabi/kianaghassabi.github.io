About

My research focuses on designing and evaluating machine learning methods for complex sequential data. I am particularly interested in multivariate time series, long-horizon forecasting, generative modeling, and practical methods for working with non-stationary systems.

I enjoy combining modeling, implementation, and interpretation—especially when a system is challenging to understand from the outside and needs careful analysis to make it work reliably in practice.
